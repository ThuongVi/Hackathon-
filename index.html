Function & Flow

01 / ECO-CYCLE

The system operates as a smart closed-loop cycle: nutrient-rich water and fish waste are processed and delivered directly to the roots, ensuring 100% organic growth.

02 / SMART MONITORING

Integrated sensors monitor pH, DO, and turbidity 24/7 to maintain a perfect environment for both fish and microgreens.

03 / AI LOGIC & DISEASE DETECTION

An AI Camera continuously tracks plant height and health status. Utilizing advanced vision algorithms, the system can detect early signs of diseases or nutrient deficiencies, while AI-driven logic autonomously regulates environment factors to ensure optimal recovery and growth. AI-driven logic autonomously regulates Full-spectrum LED intensity to optimize photosynthesis and ensure rapid recovery.

04 / OPTIMAL CROP: WATER SPINACH

Water Spinach Microgreens are the core of our system, featuring a rapid harvest cycle of only 5-7 days. They are a nutritious staple, easy to prepare for any family meal.

05 / URBAN CONNECTIVITY

Real-time status and harvest alerts are sent via Telegram, giving parents total control over their food sources remotely.

SCALABILITY

Vietnam Market Launchpad

Food safety and limited living spaces are pressing challenges in major cities like Ho Chi Minh City, Hanoi, and Da Nang. Agro Matrix's flexible Modular design allows for easy deployment in any apartment or townhouse with restricted space.

Expansion to APAC Region

Food security challenges and rapid urbanization are not unique to Vietnam but are common across Asia-Pacific (APAC) countries like Thailand, Indonesia, and the Philippines. Our model is highly adaptable to megacities like Bangkok, Jakarta, and Manila, where residents face climate change and the need for on-site clean food.

Multi-Model Development Potential

The project can scale from household units to larger models such as schools (STEM education), clean food restaurants, or Green Offices throughout the Asia-Pacific region.

International Adaptability
